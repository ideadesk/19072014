Work. Meet. Collaborate.

Affordable desks & meeting rooms for freelancers, startups & local businesses in south-west London.

One Dynamic Workspace

We provide open plan offices, drop-in business lounge and private meeting rooms on your doorstep!
Space to Work

We have two fantastic secure desk areas with balcony and patio. Full sized reserved desks with storage for residents. Unlimited hotdesking with 24/7 access for the cost conscious. All with superfast broadband (WiFi and wired) included, printing facilities, private kitchen and complimentary Nespresso coffee. Ideal? We think so

Space to Meet

We have three meeting rooms which can be used for anything from a full-blown event with food to an intimate 1-2-1. Meet your client, meet the team, build a brand, engage with your audience, all within a professional business environment just 20 minutes from Waterloo. Projection equipment, white board, flip charts, etc. available.

Space to Collaborate

It’s hard for freelancers and startups to work in isolation. Companies too need to connect; with customers, suppliers and with themselves. Collaboration is the grown-up way of working. With so much going on at Maple Works, you are sure to bump into someone who shares your interests and can help you on your way.
